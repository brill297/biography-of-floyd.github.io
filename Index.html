Early life
Floyd started playing football in 2015 joined kwaito football club as a left winger.Floyd's phenomenal skills garned the attention of RastaFC.At the age of 14 kiddo began to play for Rasta's under-14 team.He scored 10 goals in 14 games for the junior team and he quickly graduated through the higher-level teams until at the age of 16 he was given his informal debut with RastaFC in a friendly match.While growing up he always loved football his soccer idol was Cristiano Ronaldo.

Kiddo's play countinued to rapidly improve over the years.He now played for RastaFC and scored 9 goals in 21 matches during that season.Kiddo led RastaFC to win final Top8
